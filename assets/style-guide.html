Voice & tone

Honest, a little dry. Food puns are encouraged; cape-and-sword feature flexing is not.

Do

  • Lean into the wrapper / soft-shell metaphor.
  • Be self-aware — Jira is dull and that's fine.
  • Lowercase "taco" in running copy; uppercase TACO in the wordmark only.
  • Use "//" comment framing for asides.

Don't

  • Claim taco "replaces" or "fixes" Jira — it wraps it.
  • Overpromise AI magic. We did the AI; the user gets the shell.
  • Use exclamation points or hype words ("revolutionary", "10x", "delight").
  • Mix the brand yellow with non-brand accent colors.
